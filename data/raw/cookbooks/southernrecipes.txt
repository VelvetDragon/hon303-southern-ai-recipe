**SPICY PECANS**

**Ingredients:**
- 1 egg white
- 2 tablespoons water
- 1 6-oz. can pecans
- 2/3 cup sugar
- 2 teaspoons cinnamon
- 1/2 teaspoon ginger
- 1/4 cup cornstarch

**Steps:**
1. Beat egg white with water until slightly frothy.
2. Stir in pecans until well coated.
3. Combine sugar, cinnamon, ginger, and cornstarch; sift into a shallow pan.
4. Toss pecans in the sugar mixture to coat thoroughly.
5. Bake at 250°F for 1 1/2 hours, stirring occasionally.
6. Cool and store. Yields about 3/4 pound.

**MINTY GLAZED NUTS**

**Ingredients:**
- 1 1/2 cups sugar
- 1/2 cup light corn syrup
- 1/2 cup water
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 2 cups shelled walnuts
- 3 tablespoons melted butter
- 1 teaspoon peppermint extract

**Steps:**
1. Combine sugar, corn syrup, water, and salt in a saucepan.

3. Cook to hard-crack stage (300°F).
4. Remove from heat and stir in walnuts, butter, and peppermint extract.
5. Spread into a greased shallow pan.
6. Separate nuts with a fork as they cool. Yields 1 pound.
