**BRUNSWICK STEW**

**Ingredients:**
- 1 3-lb chicken
- 1 tbsp salt
- Few peppercorns
- 1 carrot
- 2 celery ribs with leaves
- 1 onion
- 1.5 quarts water
- 1 can tomatoes
- 3 medium onions, sliced thin
- 6 medium potatoes, sliced thin
- 1.5 cups fresh lima beans or 1 pkg frozen
- 2 cups fresh white corn or 1 can cream style
- 1 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tsp sugar
- 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
- Salt, to taste
- Pepper, to taste

**Steps:**
1. Steam chicken, salt, peppercorns, carrot, celery, onion, and water until chicken is very tender.
2. Strain stock, shred chicken, and return to stock.
3. Add tomatoes, potatoes, onions, lima beans, and seasonings.
4. Cook until vegetables are tender.
5. Add corn and cook 10 minutes longer.
6. Serve on split cornbread for 8–10 servings.

**SHRIMP GUMBO**

**Ingredients:**
- 0.33 cup butter
- 2 tbsp flour
- 1 medium green pepper, thinly sliced
- 2 medium onions, sliced
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 No. 2 can tomatoes
- 1 pkg frozen cut okra
- 3 beef bouillon cubes
- 4 tsp Worcestershire sauce
- 0.25 tsp ground cloves
- 0.5 tsp chili powder
- 2 bay leaves
- 1.5 tbsp salt
- 0.25 tsp pepper
- 3 cups water
- 2 lbs shelled raw shrimp
- 3 cups hot cooked rice
- 0.75 cup snipped parsley

**Steps:**
1. Melt butter in a Dutch oven, stir in flour, and cook over low heat until brown.
2. Add green pepper, onions, and garlic; cook slowly until tender.
3. Add tomatoes, bouillon cubes, Worcestershire sauce, cloves, chili powder, bay leaves, salt, pepper, and water.
4. Simmer uncovered for 15 minutes.
5. Add okra and simmer for 30 minutes.
6. Heat to boiling, add shrimp, and simmer covered until shrimp are pink and tender.
7. Remove bay leaves.
8. Toss rice with parsley, form a ring on shallow plates, and fill center with gumbo for 6–8 servings.

**BREAST OF CHICKEN ALMONDINE**

**Ingredients:**
- 1 cup uncooked rice
- 2.5 cups water
- 2 cups chopped celery
- 1 tsp instant minced onion
- 1 cup cream of mushroom soup
- 0.25 cup slivered almonds
- 2.25 tsp salt
- 3 large whole chicken breasts, halved
- 0.25 tsp pepper
- 0.66 cup milk
- 2 tbsp butter

**Steps:**
1. Cook rice with 1 tsp salt in 2 cups water; bring to a boil, then simmer covered over low heat for 14 minutes.
2. Remove from heat and let stand covered for 10 minutes.
3. Melt butter in a skillet, brown chicken breasts slowly on both sides, then drain.
4. Cook celery in 0.5 cup water, covered, until tender, about 8–10 minutes.
5. Spread rice in a greased 8x12x2-inch shallow baking dish.
6. Spread celery over rice, then place chicken skin side up.
7. Sprinkle chicken with 0.25 tsp salt, pepper, and onion.
8. Mix milk and mushroom soup, pour over chicken and rice.
9. Bake at 350°F for 45 minutes.
10. Melt 2 tbsp butter, stir in almonds to coat, and spread over soup.
11. Bake 15 minutes more until almonds are brown, for 6 servings.

**WALDORF ASTORIA RED CAKE**

**Ingredients:**
- 0.5 cup shortening
- 1.25 cups white sugar
- 2 eggs
- 0.25 tsp salt
- 1 tsp vanilla
- 2 oz red food coloring
- 1 cup buttermilk
- 2.5 cups flour
- 1 tsp baking soda
- 1 tsp vinegar
- 2 tsp cocoa
- 1 cup milk
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 cup butter
- 3 tbsp flour

**Steps:**
1. Mix vinegar and baking soda in a small cup and let stand.
2. Cream shortening, sugar, and eggs.
3. Make a paste of cocoa and some red food coloring; add to creamed mixture.
4. Add remaining coloring, then alternate buttermilk, salt, and flour.
5. Add vanilla, then soda and vinegar mixture; stir well.
6. Pour into two 8-inch pans and bake at 350°F for 30 minutes.
7. Cool and split layers in half lengthwise.
8. For frosting, cook 3 tbsp flour and milk until thick, then cool.
9. Cream 1 cup sugar and butter until fluffy, gradually add flour-milk mixture, beating constantly.
10. Spread frosting between layers and on top, for 8–10 servings.

**BLACK BOTTOM PIE**

**Ingredients:**
- 16 ginger snaps
- 0.25 cup butter or margarine
- 1 tbsp unflavored gelatin
- 0.25 cup cold water
- 1.75 cups milk
- 1 tbsp cornstarch
- 1 cup sugar
- 0.125 tsp salt
- 2 tbsp rum flavoring
- 4 eggs, separated
- 2 squares unsweetened chocolate
- 1 tsp vanilla
- 0.125 tsp almond extract
- 0.125 tsp cream of tartar
- Shaved chocolate, for garnish

**Steps:**
1. Roll ginger snaps into fine crumbs and mix with softened margarine.
2. Press into a 10-inch pie pan and bake at 300°F for 10 minutes.
3. Soak gelatin in cold water.
4. Scald milk, then mix cornstarch, 0.5 cup sugar, and salt.
5. Beat egg yolks, stir in sugar-cornstarch mixture, then add warm milk.
6. Cook over hot water until mixture coats a spoon, then add gelatin.
7. Divide custard: to one half, add melted chocolate, vanilla, and almond extract; pour into crust.
8. Cool the other half, then beat egg whites with cream of tartar until stiff, adding 0.5 cup sugar.
9. Fold cooled custard and rum flavoring into egg whites, spread over chocolate layer.
10. Sprinkle with shaved chocolate and chill for several hours, for 8 servings.
