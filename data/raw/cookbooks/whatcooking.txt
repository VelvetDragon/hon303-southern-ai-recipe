**ROASTED WILD DUCK**

- Salt and pepper

- Bacon strips (optional)
- Wild rice (optional)
- Water

**Steps:**
1. Clean, wipe, and dry ducks.

- 1/2 tsp. chili powder
- 1/8 tsp. pepper
- 4 1/2 cups tomatoes (2-1 lb. 4 oz. cans)
- 3/4 cup water
- 1 Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
- 1 lb. shrimp, cooked, shelled, and deveined
- 8 oz. long spaghetti

**Steps:**
1. Melt butter in a large, heavy skillet or saucepot.
2. Add onion, green pepper, and garlic, cook over low heat for 5 minutes.
3. Stir in flour, salt, chili powder, pepper, tomatoes, water, and Worcestershire sauce.
4. Cook until thickened, stirring constantly, then simmer uncovered for 15 minutes.
5. Cut shrimp into pieces, add to sauce, simmer 20 minutes.
6. Cook spaghetti in boiling salted water until tender (about 7 minutes), drain.
7. Serve sauce over spaghetti. Serves 6.

**CHICKEN SPAGHETTI**

**Ingredients:**
- 1 large hen
- 1 lb. Cheddar cheese
- 1 medium onion
- 1 pod garlic
- 1/2 green pepper
- 1 medium size can mushrooms
- 1 can tomatoes
- 1 large pkg. spaghetti

**Steps:**
1. Boil and bone chicken.
2. Sauté onions, garlic, and green pepper.
3. Add tomatoes, mushrooms, and half the cheese.
4. Boil spaghetti in chicken broth, then add tomato sauce and chicken.
5. Place in a casserole, top with remaining cheese.
6. Bake at 350°F for 45 minutes.

**CARAMEL KISSES**

**Ingredients:**
- 1 cup sugar
- 1/2 cup dark corn syrup
- 1 Tbsp. butter
- 1 tsp. vinegar
- 1/2 tsp. baking soda
- 1 tsp. vanilla

**Steps:**
1. Combine sugar, corn syrup, butter, and vinegar in a saucepan.
2. Cook to hard crack stage (300°F).
3. Remove from heat, add baking soda and vanilla, stir well.
4. Pour into a buttered pan, cool slightly, pull until light and porous.
5. Cut into pieces, wrap in waxed paper.

**DIVINITY FUDGE**

**Ingredients:**
- 2 cups sugar
- 1/2 cup white corn syrup
- 1/2 cup water
- 2 egg whites
- 1 tsp. vanilla
- 1 cup chopped nuts

**Steps:**
1. Boil sugar, corn syrup, and water to soft ball stage (235°F).
2. Beat egg whites until stiff.
3. Pour half the syrup slowly over egg whites, beating constantly.
4. Cook remaining syrup to hard ball stage (250°F).
5. Pour over egg white mixture, beat until creamy.
6. Add vanilla and nuts, drop by spoonfuls onto waxed paper.

**CREAMY PRALINES**

**Ingredients:**
- 2 cups sugar

- 2 Tbsp. white corn syrup
- 2 Tbsp. butter
- 1 tsp. vanilla
- 2 cups pecans

**Steps:**

**PECAN BARS**

**Ingredients:**
- **Crust:**
  - 1/3 cup butter or oleo
  - 1/2 cup brown sugar
  - 1 1/2 cups sifted flour
  - 1/2 tsp. baking powder
  - 1/4 cup finely chopped pecans
- **Topping:**
  - 2 eggs
  - 3/4 cup white corn syrup
  - 1/4 cup brown sugar
  - 3 Tbsp. flour
  - 1/2 tsp. salt
  - 1 tsp. vanilla
  - 3/4 cup chopped pecans

**Steps:**
1. Cream butter and brown sugar, blend in flour, baking powder, and pecans.
2. Press into an oblong cake pan, bake for 10 minutes.
3. Beat eggs until light, stir in corn syrup, sugar, flour, salt, and vanilla.
4. Spread over baked crust, sprinkle with pecans.
5. Bake until firm.

**BRAZIL NUT CARAMELS**

**Ingredients:**
- 3/4 cup chopped Brazil nuts
- 1 lb. vanilla caramels
- 2 Tbsp. shortening

**Steps:**
1. Spread chopped Brazil nuts on a sheet of foil.

**HOT TEA PUNCH**

**Ingredients:**
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 stick cinnamon
- 1 cup orange juice
- 4 Tbsp. lemon juice
- 2 tsp. grated lemon rind
- 3 tsp. grated orange rind
- 1 cup pineapple juice
- 1 - 13 oz. can crushed pineapple
- 6 Tbsp. tea
- 6 cups boiling water for tea
- 1 cup boiling water

**Steps:**
1. Make tea, steep for 5 minutes, strain.
2. Pour 1 cup boiling water over sugar, cinnamon, lemon, and orange rind, steep a few minutes.
3. Add tea, orange juice, lemon juice, pineapple juice, and crushed pineapple.
4. Let stand a few hours or overnight.
5. Serve hot. Serves 16-18.

**EGG NOG**

**Ingredients:**
- 12 egg yolks
- 12 Tbsp. sugar
- 1 1/2 cups Bourbon whiskey
- 12 egg whites, stiffly beaten
- 1 1/2 pints cream, whipped
- Nutmeg (optional)

**Steps:**
1. Beat egg yolks until light, gradually add sugar.
2. Slowly add Bourbon whiskey, beating constantly.
3. Fold in stiffly beaten egg whites and whipped cream.
4. Serve with a dash of nutmeg on each cup if desired. Makes 1 gallon.

**BARBECUE SAUCE**

**Ingredients:**
- 1/2 cup sweet pickle vinegar
- 1/4 cup water
- 1 bouillon cube
- 2 Tbsp. sugar
- 1/4 lb. butter
- 2 tsp. salt
- 1 Tbsp. prepared mustard
- 1 tsp. black pepper
- 1/4 tsp. cayenne pepper
- 1 medium onion, minced
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- Juice of 1 lemon
- 3 Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
- 1 cup ketchup

**Steps:**
1. Simmer vinegar, water, bouillon cube, sugar, butter, salt, mustard, black pepper, cayenne, onion, garlic, and lemon juice until onions and garlic are soft.
2. Add Worcestershire sauce and ketchup, bring to a boil.
3. Remove from heat immediately.
